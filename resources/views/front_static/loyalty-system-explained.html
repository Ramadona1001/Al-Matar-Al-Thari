Loyalty Points System Explained

Everything you need to know about earning and redeeming loyalty points

How to Earn Points

Shopping at Partner Merchants

When you make a purchase at any partner merchant, you automatically earn points based on your purchase amount.

Rate: 1 point for every $1 spent

Example: If you spend $50, you earn 50 points.

Scanning QR Codes

Scan the QR code at partner stores to earn bonus points on top of your regular purchase points.

Bonus: 10-50 extra points depending on the promotion

Referring Friends

When someone signs up using your referral code and makes their first purchase, you earn bonus points.

Bonus: 100 points per successful referral

Special Promotions

Participate in special events, seasonal promotions, and limited-time offers to earn extra points.

Bonus: Varies by promotion (usually 2x or 3x points)

How to Redeem Points

Discount at Checkout

Use your points to get discounts when making purchases at partner merchants.

Conversion: 100 points = $1 discount

Example: Use 500 points to get $5 off your purchase.

Cashback

Convert your points to cashback that will be credited to your account balance.

Conversion: 100 points = $1 cashback

Minimum: 1000 points required for cashback redemption

Exclusive Rewards

Redeem points for exclusive rewards, gift cards, and special items available only to members.

Variety: Gift cards, merchandise, and special experiences

Points Validity

Expiration Policy

Your loyalty points are valid for 12 months from the date they were earned.

Important: Points expire if not used within 12 months. Use them before they expire!
  • Points earned on Jan 1, 2024 expire on Jan 1, 2025
  • You'll receive email notifications 30 days before expiration
  • Check your account dashboard to see point expiration dates

Calculation Example

Example Scenario

Scenario:

You make a purchase of $200 at a partner merchant.

Points Earned:
  • Base points: $200 × 1 = 200 points
  • QR code bonus: +25 points
  • Special promotion (2x points): +200 points
Total Points Earned: 425 points
Redeemable Value:

425 points = $4.25 discount or $4.25 cashback

Next Purchase:

If you use these 425 points on a $50 purchase, you pay only $45.75

Tips to Maximize Your Points

Shop Regularly

Make regular purchases to accumulate points faster

Watch for Promotions

Subscribe to notifications for bonus point opportunities

Always Scan QR

Don't forget to scan QR codes for bonus points

Use Before Expiry

Check expiration dates and use points before they expire
