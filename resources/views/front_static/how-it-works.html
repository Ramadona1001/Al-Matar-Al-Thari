How It Works

How to Use as a Customer

1
Sign up and create your account

Register with your email and phone number. Verification takes just a few minutes.

2
Shop at partner merchants

Visit any store that displays our logo or QR code at the checkout counter.

3
Earn loyalty points with every purchase

Scan the QR code or provide your phone number to earn points automatically.

4
Redeem points for discounts and rewards

Use accumulated points to get discounts on future purchases or exchange for cashback.

How to Use as a Merchant

1
Register your business

Complete the merchant registration form with your business details and documents.

2
Set up your offers and discounts

Create attractive offers, set discount percentages, and define validity periods.

3
Attract new customers

Your offers will be visible to all platform users, helping you reach more customers.

4
Track sales and analytics

Monitor your performance through the merchant dashboard with real-time analytics.

QR Code Usage

Simply scan the QR code at partner stores to earn points instantly. No need for physical cards or complex processes.

  • Quick & Easy: Scan and earn points in seconds
  • No Cards Needed: Everything is digital
  • Instant Updates: Points appear in your account immediately
